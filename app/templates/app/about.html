About us

Uwi-Shi celebrates the fusion of Japanese and Filipino cultures, infusing the elegance and precision of Japanese cuisine with the warmth and comfort of Filipino flavors.

Our Mission

To bring the joy of creating and savoring delicious make-your-own bento boxes to our customer's homes. Combining the essence of Japanese culinary excellence and the warmth of Filipino hospitality, we aim to provide a seamless e-commerce experience that offers a wide variety of fresh ingredients and allows individuals to customize their bento boxes to their liking. Through our reliable delivery and convenient pick-up services, we strive to make the process of enjoying a delectable and personalized bento box a delightful and accessible experience for everyone. We are dedicated to fostering a sense of culinary adventure, promoting cultural appreciation, and ensuring customer satisfaction in every bite.

Background

Uwi-Shi is founded on the belief that food should be both delicious and customizable to suit individual preferences. The concept was inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Japan and the Philippines, two cultures renowned for their flavorful and diverse cuisines. The founders recognized the growing demand for convenient and personalized dining experiences, especially in urban areas where people lead busy lives.
